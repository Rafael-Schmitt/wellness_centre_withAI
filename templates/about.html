Our Story

Founded in 2015, Serenity Wellness began as a simple dream: to create a sanctuary where people could reconnect with themselves through nature's healing power.

Nestled in the breathtaking landscape of Santa Catarina, Brazil, our center emerged from a deep belief in the transformative power of tropical environments. We witnessed how the combination of ocean air, lush rainforests, and warm sunlight created the perfect setting for holistic healing.

What started as a small beachfront meditation group has blossomed into a comprehensive wellness retreat, attracting visitors from around the world who seek peace, restoration, and transformation.
